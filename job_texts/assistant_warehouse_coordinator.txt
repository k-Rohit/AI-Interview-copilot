SUMMARY:
The Assistant Warehouse Coordinator will work closely with the Warehouse Manager to oversee the daily operations of The Creme Shop warehouse facility. This individual will assist with inventory management, coordinate shipping and receiving activities, and ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards. The successful candidate will possess strong organizational skills, a keen attention to detail, and the ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment. Work hours are from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm, Monday to Friday. There is flexibility to work evenings, weekends, and overtime occasionally. ​ **Bilingual Korean/English or Spanish/English candidate is required** ​
RESPONSIBILITIES:
· Assist with the receipt, inspection, and storage of incoming shipments. · Coordinate order fulfillment processes, including picking, packing, and shipping of customer orders. · Maintain accurate inventory records and conduct regular cycle counts to ensure inventory accuracy. · Assist in organizing warehouse space and optimizing storage layouts for efficient operations. · Collaborate with other team members to streamline warehouse processes and improve productivity. · Ensure compliance with company policies and procedures, as well as safety and regulatory requirements. · Operate warehouse equipment, such as forklifts and pallet jacks, in a safe and efficient manner. · Assist with training and supervising warehouse staff as needed. · Provide support for special projects and initiatives as assigned by management.
REQUIREMENTS:
· High school diploma or equivalent; additional education or certification in logistics or related field preferred. · Previous experience working in a warehouse or distribution center environment preferred. · Strong organizational skills and the ability to prioritize tasks effectively. · Excellent attention to detail and accuracy in data entry and record-keeping. · Proficiency in using Microsoft Office applications. · Ability to work independently with minimal supervision and collaborate effectively in a team environment. · Strong communication skills, both verbal and written. · Ability to lift and carry heavy objects up to 50 lbs, climb ladders, and operate warehouse equipment safely. · Flexibility to work occasional evenings, weekends, or overtime as needed. The role of Assistant Warehouse Coordinator is essential in ensuring the smooth and efficient operation of the Creme Shop warehouse facility. If you have a strong interest in logistics, motivation to succeed, and a desire to work as part of a dynamic team, we urge you to apply for this exciting opportunity.
