SUMMARY:
Cashier is responsible in assisting the retail store management team with servicing customers, procuring products, marketing and helping in administering and assisting instore related functions and activities. Assists in the processing of all Representative orders and payments. Helps the store coordinators in all the daily activities of the cashiering team . Ensure that each customer receives outstanding service by providing a friendly environment, which includes greeting and acknowledging every customer, maintaining outstanding standards, solid product knowledge, store promotions, and all other aspects of customer service.
RESPONSIBILITIES:
Deep understanding of products, promotions, and advertisements. Deep understanding of Company Policies and Store Policies Ensures that purchasing customer is authorized to purchase at the Sales Center. Ensure that the picture and account number on the ID card matches the screen and Representative that they are assisting. Cashiers associate will be required to know the current campaigns, demos, and flyer specials. Count and verify at the start of every shift their opening cash amount provided by the Store Coordinator. Cashier associate will carefully bag products, double bag heavy items, and will be vigilant and remind the Representative to take all the bags pertaining to their purchase. Cashier associates will “Thanks” every customer for shopping at the Sales Center.
REQUIREMENTS:
High School Diploma/G.E.D. or equivalent experience. At least 1 year experience in a warehouse environment. Bilingual - Spanish preferred
